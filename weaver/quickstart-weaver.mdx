## 1. The Entrance Ritual

Materialize your identity sigil via the **Magic Sigil** (passwordless) or the **Identity Portal**. Open **http://localhost:3000/canvas** after `bun dev`. Verify environment planes at **/observatory** if anything fails to connect.

## 2. Manifesting Instruments

Open the **Palette Shard** [Left HUD] or use the **Sidebar** on canvas. Drag-and-drop a **Unit Persona** or **Logic Gate** onto the **Spatial Loom**.

- **Visual Cue**: Watch for the **Drop Zone Pulse** to confirm substrate readiness.
- **Magnetic Snap**: Instruments will align to the 20px grid automatically.

## 3. Weaving Leylines

Drag from a unit's **Source Handle** (Right) to another unit's **Target Handle** (Left).

- **LeyLines**: These animated synapses carry metabolic signal packets.
- **Directionality**: Logic flows from Genesis to Convergence.

## 4. Scrying the Genome

Every unit has a hidden logical kernel.

- **Ritual**: Click the **ScanEye** icon on a node to perform a **Veil-Peel**.
- **Forensics**: Scry traits like **Stochasticity**, **Rigor**, and **Persistence**. Recalibrate these values to mutate unit behavior.

## 5. Engaging the Swarm

Once your topology is stable, click **ENGAGE** on the **Sovereign dock** or type `/run` in the command bar.

- **Thinking Ghosting**: Units will manifest a blue blur-aura during "Slow Thinking" cycles.
- **The Chronicle**: Read raw signals in the **Signal Chronicle** [Bottom HUD]; **AI Debug** tab calls Nexus `/developer/debug-assist`.
- **AgentFS Codex**: Dock → AgentFS Codex for sandbox files (pull/push/checkpoint).

## 6. Registries

**Sidebar → Registries** opens agents, skills, toolkits, automations, APM, and more without leaving the canvas.
